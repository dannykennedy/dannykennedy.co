There's a lot of systems in the world. Ecosystems, political systems, design systems. I've decided that I'm into 'symbolic systems'. That cuts across languages, programming, and anything that operates with symbols, signs and codes. Specifically, I love full stack web development, linguistics, art, writing and a lot of languages - from German and Persian to Japanese and Kunwinjku. Oh and I also love dreams. The original symbolic system. All these systems are intricate and fascinating, but in the end they exist to communicate things. The big things that are important to people. So I'm a big fan of making things, and trying to do things that are meaningful in general.
